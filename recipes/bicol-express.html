Bicol Express

Bicol Express is a creamy stew that combines spice and sweetness in a well-balanced manner. The dish makes use of ginger and local seasonings resulting in a flavorful combination of meat and sauce.

INGREDIENTS

  • Coconut milk
  • Pork/Chicken meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green chili
  • Red chili
  • Bagoong (Shrimp paste)

STEPS:

  1. Cook the meat until golden brown.
  2. After cooking the meat, saut√© the onion and the garlic.
  3. Add bagoong and cook for a little.
  4. Pour the coconut milk and let it simmer
  5. Last, add your chilis, and viola!!!
