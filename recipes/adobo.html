Chicken Adobo

Chicken Adobo is a straightforward adobong manok recipe wherein chicken pieces are cooked using the inadobo method without marinating the ingredients. This is my basic method in cooking adobo. It is what I always refer to when cooking this dish on a busy day. Marinating the chicken in soy sauce, sometimes in both soy…

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken meat
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Chili (Red or Green)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Bayleaf

STEPS:

  1. Sear the meat until golden brown.
  2. Add your onion and garlic to sauté.
  3. Pour soy sauce and vinegar, and let it cook for a while.
  4. Last, add bayleaf and water, and let it simmer.
